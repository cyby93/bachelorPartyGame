## Session — 2026-04-21, UpgradeConfig rewrite + upgrade simulation

**Goal:** Prepare for Illidan fight tuning by understanding player power at L6 entry.

**Cyby's design decisions confirmed:**
- 6 upgrades total per player entering L6
- Skill damage values do NOT scale with R — flat absolute values, intentional

**What I found:**

Three bugs in UpgradeConfig vs current SkillDatabase:
1. Warrior S2: UpgradeConfig had Charge (DASH) deltas — SkillDatabase has Bladestorm. `distance` delta created dead field.
2. Hunter S1: UpgradeConfig had Multi-Shot deltas (`damage`, `projectileCount`) — SkillDatabase has Aimed Shot (CAST). Wrong paths.
3. Hunter S2: UpgradeConfig had `petStats.damage`/`petStats.hp` paths — SkillDatabase uses `beastVariants[]` array. Paths didn't resolve.

Scale mismatch: SkillDatabase had been significantly reworked (much lower base values) since UpgradeConfig was written. Old deltas were sized for higher base values, producing ×2.8–11.1 DPS multipliers at full focus. Target was ~×1.5.

**What I did:**

1. Wrote `tools/dps-calculator-upgraded.js` — runs base / focus (3+3+0+0) / spread (2+2+1+1) scenarios per class.
2. Rewrote `shared/UpgradeConfig.js` from scratch against current SkillDatabase values.
   - Targeted ×1.5–1.9 per individual skill at tier 3 (integer damage deltas, no fractional values)
   - Fixed all three structural bugs
   - Hunter S2 (Call of the Wild): beast damage upgrades deferred — require server-side beastVariants scaling. Upgrades are duration/cooldown (uptime) only.
   - All utility skill upgrades preserved (Shield Wall, Blink, Fear, Death Grip, Sprint, Vanish, Bloodlust, Frost Nova, Anti-Magic Shell, Mass Rez, PW:Shield, Regrowth, Tranquility, Chain Heal, Bloodlust)

**Results after rewrite:**

| Scenario | Avg ×mult | Est. fight time |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Base (no upgrades) | ×1.00 | 8.0 min |
| Spread 2+2+1+1 | ×1.42 | ~5.6 min |
| Focus 3+3 (best 2) | ×1.70 | ~4.7 min |

Per-class focus multipliers: ×1.54–1.91. All within target range.

**Call of the Wild beast damage fix (same session):**
- Root cause: `beastVariants[]` array can't be pathed by dot-notation delta system.
- Fix: added `damageBonus: 0` scalar to Call of the Wild in SkillDatabase. SkillSystem.js line 722 now applies it: `damage: chosen.damage + (config.damageBonus ?? 0)`. UpgradeConfig updated to use `{ damageBonus: 1/1/2 }` per tier (+4 total at T3).
- Simulator still shows Hunter ⚠ (WILD_BEAST not modelled by sim), but upgrades work in-game.

**Next step:**
